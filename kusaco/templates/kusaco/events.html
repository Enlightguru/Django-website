1. WEEKLY MEETINGS.

venue: KU BSSC

time : 6.45pm - 8.00pm

date : Every Thursday

KUSACO holds weekly meetings every Thursday evening from 6.45pm to 8.00pm at Kenyatta University Students Business Center(BSSC) room 152. Each Thusrday a new topic on either HIV, Gender equality , Stigmatization, Drug and Substance abuse, Relationships and Dating and many other intresting fields is tabled down and members given a chance to discuss and give their views. At the end of the discussions all the views are evaluated and the members together settle on the best views that were presented. These discussions have for a long time held a reputation of raising competent debators, open minded thinkers and great Orators. The self esteem of various members has also been raised by just attending and participating in these discussions.Lots of Our members have also shown intrest in research which makes the raises the interlectual standards of the discussions that we have. These are meetings you cant afford to miss, set aside your Thursday evenings, come have fun but at the same time learn alot.
